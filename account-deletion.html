Account Deletion – YDB (You Deserve Better)

If you wish to delete your YDB account and associated personal and health data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Delete Your Account

You may delete your account directly within the app:

Profile → Settings → Delete Account

Alternatively, you may request deletion by emailing:

privacy@youdeservebetter.in

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Account information (name, email, profile details)
  • Health assessment and tracking data
  • Medical Vault documents
  • Community posts (deleted or anonymised)

Data Retention

All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of verified deletion request.

For questions, contact privacy@youdeservebetter.in.
